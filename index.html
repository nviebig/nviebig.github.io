Hi there, I'm Niklas.

Incoming Computational Meteorologist @ Metera · Physics MSc @ ETH Zürich · Planetary & Climate Science · Visiting Researcher @ University of Oxford

I'm passionate about understanding and modeling environmental systems from atmospheric dynamics and climate feedbacks to land-use interactions and ecosystem responses. Environmental modeling sits at the intersection of physics, computational science, and data-driven methods. It's where we can ask "what if" questions about our planet's future.

My work in this space spans automatic differentiation for climate model calibration, Bayesian inference on wildfire dynamics, geospatial analysis of land management, and high-performance computing for Earth system simulations. I'm particularly drawn to problems where rigorous physics meets machine learning making simulators differentiable, learning from observations, and improving our predictive capabilities.
